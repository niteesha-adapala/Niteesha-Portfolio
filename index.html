About

Turning Data into Discoveries!

Hi, I’m Niteesha Adapala. I have a Master’s in Bioinformatics Data Science from the University of Delaware, where I discovered my love for solving biological problems with code. With a background in computational biology and experience in both academia and industry, I’ve worked on everything from protein modeling and antigen prediction to RNA-seq analysis and machine learning for disease risk.

I enjoy building efficient, scalable pipelines and exploring the intersection of biology and data science. Whether it’s working with tools like Rosetta, GROMACS, and TensorFlow, or diving into a new dataset, I’m always curious and eager to learn.

When I’m not coding or modeling proteins, you’ll probably find me experimenting with a new recipe or watching sci-fi shows. I’m always open to exciting collaborations, so feel free to browse my work.
