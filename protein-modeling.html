Protein Modeling of Influenza Hemagglutinin

This project involves modeling structural variants of the influenza hemagglutinin (HA) protein to study the effects of mutations using Rosetta and GROMACS.

Objectives

  • Model wild-type and mutant HA protein structures
  • Simulate dynamics and stability using molecular dynamics
  • Evaluate changes in hydrogen bonding and conformational energy

Tools Used

  • Rosetta – protein folding & structure prediction
  • GROMACS – molecular dynamics simulation
  • PyMOL – structure visualization

Key Results

The simulations helped identify the most stable conformers and visualize differences in hydrogen-bond networks across mutated HA variants.
