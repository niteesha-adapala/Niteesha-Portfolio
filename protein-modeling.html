Protein Modeling of Influenza Hemagglutinin

This project involves modeling structural variants of the influenza hemagglutinin (HA) protein using Rosetta and GROMACS.

  • Tools: Rosetta, GROMACS, PyMOL
  • Goal: Analyze how mutations affect the protein's folding and stability.
  • Results: Predicted the most stable conformers and visualized hydrogen-bond networks.
